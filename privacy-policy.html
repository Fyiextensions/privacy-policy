1. Overview

ComiFix - Emotion Detector is a Chrome extension that detects user emotions via webcam and plays relaxing media to improve mood. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This extension does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

2. Data Collection and Usage

  • Camera Access: The extension accesses your webcam only with your explicit permission to detect emotions in real-time.
  • No video feed, images, or audio are recorded, stored, or transmitted.
  • All detection happens locally in your browser.
  • No Personal Data: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or user activity.
  • No Ads or Cookies: This extension does not use advertising services or tracking cookies.
